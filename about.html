The idea for Protest Parrot came from our group wanting to create
a twitter bot that could search tweets for images that have
protesters faces and report them to protect protesters privacy.
As over the past weeks, the police and other government agencies
have been identifying protestors through the photos and videos
uploaded to social media sites. Our bot scans tweets related to
BLM protests and replies under them that faces may be detected
or recognizable in the photo which alerts users to delete or report the photo.




The Flaming Flamingos' Twitter Hackathon Project. Protest Parrot! protects your privacy and provides protest updates.
